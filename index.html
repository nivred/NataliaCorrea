About Me

A Health Coach is a highly educated guide in the fields of nutrition, wellness, bio-individuality and mentoring, who supports clients to achieve their health goals through individualized food changes and lifestyle and behavior adjustments.

As a Health Coach, I emphasize holistic health beyond the plate, I approach wellness through 12 primary areas of your life that impact your long-term health just as much as the food you eat (like your relationships, career, spirituality, and physical activity). I also focus on bio-individuality, the idea that we’re all different and have unique dietary, lifestyle, emotional, and physical needs. This means that I don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to health and wellness. Instead, I work with clients to help them discover how to fuel their bodies and become the healthiest, happiest versions of themselves through a holistic approach.

    Are you...
  • struggling with emotional and binge eating/overeating?
  • always fighting against your body because you're afraid to lose control?
  • tired of following diet plans only to gain the weight back and return to binging/overeating?
  • experiencing food fear, guilt, shame, or regret after eating your favorite foods?
  • constantly comparing your body to the people around you just to end-up feeling like you don’t look good enough?
  • feeling like your relationship with food and your body is holding you back from following your dreams and showing up in the world as your real self?
  • constantly thinking about food to the point where you’re starting to feel like food is dominating your life?
  • ready to let go of the diet mentality and build a healthy relationship with food and your body?
    Now, imagine if you...
  • were supported every step of the way to build a healthy relationship with food and with your body?
  • were able to understand and manage your emotions without having to use food?
  • could recognize and honor your cravings without guilt and shame?
  • let go of all food rules and food fears and live a more flexible and enjoyable life?
  • reconnect with your intuitive wisdom and be able to listen to your body's needs?
  • make peace with your body and make it your HOME?
  • prioritize your wellbeing & learn how to nourish yourself to make intuitive eating your lifestyle?
  • could connect with others, without stressing about being judged for what you look like or what you put into your mouth?

Food Freedom Framework

  • Here we will decode your food story
  • Recognizing the food fears, rules, beliefs, stereotypes, standards, and biases that are keeping stuck in the restriction cycle.
  • Challenge them and discover which ones support you in your life and which ones are no longer serving you.
  • You will discover why you remain stuck between control and lack of control of food, and we will work on it so that you can gain more stability.
  • Be prepared to let go of the old stories that have been holding you back from feeling at peace with your food and your body and get ready to create new ones that truly support you.
  • Here you will learn how to listen to your body again
  • You will become aware about the undeniable connection between food and emotions, and how that connection shows up in your life
  • Reconnect with you hunger and satiation cues
  • Decode your cravings and honor them without feeling like they are controlling you
  • Recognize and understand the different types of hunger you can experience and learn how to satisfy them without guilt
  • Here you will understand your inner cycles and how those impact relationships with food
  • Make changes in your daily lifestyle to feel more comfortable in your body and more at peace with food
  • Implement new strategies to help you cultivate calm and find balance with food
  • Stop fighting against your body. Instead you’ll learn how to build a trust bond between you and your body
  • Establish healthy boundaries that serve you
  • Listen to your intuition and live a more fulfilled life
  • Find your middle ground and make intuitive eating your new lifestyle
  • Practice self-care on a daily basis and attract the life you desire

Participants are accepted based on pre-determined criteria such as health risk factors and participant willingness to meet all program completion requirements. Additionally, there are certain medical conditions which will disqualify enrollees from participating.

The program is an online program so there are no meetings in person. You can watch your lessons and interact with the program whenever it is convenient for you.

The lessons will take 30-45 minutes to watch each week. Each lesson is broken up into multiple short videos so you can watch whenever it is convenient to your schedule. Each lesson is available weekly so all your weekly videos should be watched within the next 7 days in order to stay on track. (We know life gets busy! So, don’t worry. You can make up lessons 1 through 20 any time.)
